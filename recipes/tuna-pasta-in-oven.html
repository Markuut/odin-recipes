Tuna Pasta In The Oven

Back to main page

A simple recipe for a tuna pasta with tomato flavor thats suitable for the whole family. This recipe is to make and and pasta are added without boiling them first.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (100g) - Onion
  • 2 - garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tbsp - oil
  • 1 packet (200g / 150g) - Pirkka tunapieces in oil
  • 1 packet (500g) - Pirkka pasteurized tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 dl - water
  • 2 tsp - Pirkka pizzaspice
  • 1 tsp - salt
  • 1/2 tsp - black pepper
  • 250g - fusilli whole wheat pasta
  • 1 packet (2dl) - Pirkka cheese cream
    • On the surface
  • 2 dl (approx. 100g) - Pirkka emmental-mozzarella grated cheese

Steps to make:

  1. Slice onion and garlic. Cook till golden on a pan.
  2. Add tuna ja tomato. Rinse the tomato can with water to get all the tomato pieces and the needed water. Add the spices. Mix and cook till boiling.
  3. Put the pasta in a oven tray (approx. 2l).
  4. Pour the tomato sauce with the pasta in the oven tray and mix.
  5. Pour the cheese cream in the and mix lightly. Sprinkle the grated cheese on the surface.
  6. Cook in the 175C oven on the lower level for 45 minutes. After taking it out, let it cool for 10 minutes.
